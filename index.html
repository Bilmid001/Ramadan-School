About Us

Our program is committed to delivering comprehensive Islamic education aimed at nurturing well-rounded young adults. We strive to equip them with not only strong religious knowledge but also critical thinking skills and good moral character. We provide flexible learning options through both online and physical classes, making education accessible regardless of location or schedule. Our regular classes, we host live guest lectures on Islamic jurisprudence and also as Daura. This unique opportunity allows students to engage directly with scholars and broaden their understanding beyond textbooks. Our program is more than just classes it’s a vibrant community that supports continuous learning, spiritual growth, and personal development. We offer workshops, study circles, and various activities that encourage students to apply Islamic teachings in their daily lives.

By Ibn Hajar Al-Asqalaniy

Bulugh al-Maram by Ibn Hajar Al-Asqalani is a fundamental hadith collection focusing on key texts used in Islamic jurisprudence, bridging classical knowledge with practical guidance for students and scholars alike.

Qur'an Tafseer

Minhaj Firqatun Najiya

Muhammad Bn Jamil Zainuu

Minhaj Firqatun Najiya provides clear guidance on Islamic beliefs and practices, focusing on the path of the saved sect and helping readers understand core aspects of faith and worship.

Tuhfatus-Saniyya

Muhammad Muhyideen A.

Tuhfatus-Saniyya is a renowned classical text offering deep insights into Islamic creed and theology, widely studied for its clear explanations and authentic teachings.

Musdalahul Hadith

Qur'an Recitation
